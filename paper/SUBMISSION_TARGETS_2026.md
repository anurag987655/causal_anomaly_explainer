# Submission Targets and Claim Language (2026)

## Safe Claim Set (Use Verbatim Style)
1. We provide a leakage-aware and reproducible evaluation pipeline for flow-based anomaly detection.
2. We show strong in-distribution detection with interpretable RCA outputs.
3. We quantify a precision vs cross-dataset robustness tradeoff under explicit model/threshold policy changes.
4. We report limitations for temporal shift and low-support attack families.

## Do Not Claim
1. State-of-the-art superiority without matched external baselines.
2. Production-grade generalization across non-CICIDS environments.
3. Consistent performance across all attack families.

## Venue Strategy
1. Workshop/short-paper security-ML venues first (fast feedback loop).
2. Then a journal extension with stronger external baseline comparisons and temporal split improvements.

## Immediate Submission Checklist
1. Finalize abstract and contributions in `paper/PAPER_READY_MANUSCRIPT.md`.
2. Include Figures 1-5 and captions from `paper/FIGURE_CAPTIONS.md`.
3. Attach reproducibility commands and artifact paths.
4. Keep one headline configuration and treat alternate policies as ablation.
5. Add ethics/deployment-risk paragraph (false positives, analyst load, misuse).
